How It Works

Self-smart technologies working together to keep society safe again.

Our Emergency Widget

Install our widget and upload phone contacts of your 3 most trusted friends who should be alerted in times of emergencies. Press the appropriate button on the widget in times of trouble and be assured of immediate help coming your way.

24/7 Monitoring

Operators in our emergency control centers work 24/7 to monitor and dispatch appropriate security personnels to the exact locations where help is needed. Our self-smart systems automatically detect anomalies to raise alerts when something suspicious happens.

Other Smart Gadgets

You can't always touch your phone in some emergencies. For this reason, we have produced many smart internet-enabled gadgets to secure your home, children, automobiles, and other valuable properties that work independent of your phone. All our gadgets are linked to your account to keep you safe.
